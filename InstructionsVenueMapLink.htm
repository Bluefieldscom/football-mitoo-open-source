Open a separate browser window for the streetmap website - click here to do this.

Locate the yellow search area - as shown below - and enter the postcode or street name of the venue in this input area.

 

Once you have found the correct map you should click with the left mouse button on the exact location of the venue. This will point a red arrow at the location.

When you are satisfied with the arrow position look under the map. See below.

Place your mouse over the "http:// etc. " link information shown in blue and the arrow will turn into a finger pointing hand.

Click the right mouse button and then choose "copy shortcut" or "copy link location" from the dropdown menu.

Now go back to the football.mitoo Venue screen for this location and paste into the Map URL field.

Finally, go to the very end of this field and change Z=1 to Z=2

This will give you a larger map.

If you prefer to use the MultiMap website instead of Streetmap then follow a similar procedure.
