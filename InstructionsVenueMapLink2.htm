Open a separate browser window for the streetmap website - click here to do this.

Enter the postcode or street name of the venue in this input area and click on GO.

Click on the Move arrow icon (it will change colour) and then on the position on the map where the arrow should be.

 

Place your mouse over the "http:// etc. " link information shown in Link to this map, copy this address

Right mouse click and copy the address.

Now go back to the football.mitoo Venue screen for this location and paste into the Map URL field.
