My name is Trevor Shaw. I have for 43 years run Europe's only football repair business.

 

As you will know, good footballs (Mitre, Nike, Adidas) cost the amateur clubs between £30 - £80 per ball. Having played amateur and professional football for over 30 years, I do know the financial problems most clubs face. I have for the past 43 years borne this in mind, and endeavoured to help the clubs with the service offered from my business.　I have　also earned a　good living from this.

 

My business is unique in the respect that no one else can offer this help to the clubs. I have also for many years repaired the footballs for sport shops throughout the UK. Unfortunately due to pressure of work I stopped doing repairs for those shops, this affected clubs around the country with no one to do the repairs, so if a ball was punctured, the clubs had no alternative　but to buy a new one, which they could ill afford. I am now about to retire. I　have no sons to pass the craft on to so I have decided to teach 5 or 6 people this superb home based business.

 

Each person I teach will have his or her own area, with no competition. I was going to offer this opportunity on the internet but with hindsight, I think it would be better to offer my business to people involved with football who obviously love the game and who would like a small business they can run from home. If anyone from your club would be interested in having a fantastic business, they can run for many years to come, they can view my website www.sportsballuk.co.uk to learn more about this craft.

 

I have also added the repairs to the American footballs to the business, as I have recently received a lot of emails from American football clubs here in the UK. One club alone has over 50 balls needing new bladders, so this market is going to be huge. I can however only teach one person from each area of the country.

 

Thank you for your time, and good luck for the season.
