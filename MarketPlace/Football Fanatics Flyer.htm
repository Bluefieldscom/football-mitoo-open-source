IT’S KICKING OFF @ VILLA PARK…

 

Football Fanatics in aid of Barnardo’s
Holte End (Suite, Concourse & Car Parks), Villa Park, Birmingham
Saturday, 27th March
Doors open 11am – 4pm

Family Pass is £10 for 2 adults, 2 children
**Included in the ticket price is 1 FREE star signing and 500 FREE striker points to spend on activities during the day or a FREE Exclusive England Football Figure

If you love football, there’s only one place to be on the 27th March and that’s Villa Park, Birmingham for the first ever Football Fanatics Fun Day in aid of Barnardos!

Whether it’s playing, supporting or collecting, if you’re a Football Fanatic, you’re sure to have a fun filled day! On the day attractions include;

·Special Guest appearances 

Whether you fancy taking penalty kicks with your Villa heroes or getting something signed by 1966 World Cup winner George Cohen, Everton & Wales legend Neville Southall or Man Utd hero Alex Stepney, you're sure to meet some football legends at Villa Park.

·The FA Cup

Hold aloft the FA Cup for the camera just like footballing legends such as Bryan Robson, Alan Hansen, Roy Keane, Dennis Wise and Patrick Vieira!

·Memorabilia Marketplace

Wide range of exhibitors selling everything that’s football, from Corinthains to programmes, plus a lot more. We’ve taken great care to ensure they’re something for everyone, so as well as sporting items, you’ll find all sorts of TV, Movie, Music and Sporting memorabilia for sale. TABLES STILL AVAILABLE FOR EXHIBITORS £29 for 1 table and £55 for 2 tables

Dealers already confirmed include….

Corinthian Paradise, Metronomebeat, AlexSports, Kooltoys, Star Signings, PLUS many more!

 

 

·Kids Zone & Inflatable Fun

From our fantastic inflatable football table, bouncy castle and striker target to face painting, crafts and games, you can bring your family with you to this event and know that they will be busy all day!

·Villa Park Stadium Tour

Get behind the scenes at Villa Park with the club’s fantastic stadium tour. See the dressing rooms and step on the hallowed turf.

·Score for Barnardo’s

We’re inviting hundreds of teams of all age groups from all over the country to show off their penalty taking skills and compete against each other to become the first ever Football Fanatics Trophy winners!

·Win great prizes!

The unique Striker point system we’re operating at Football Fanatics means that you can win additional Striker points at many of our stalls and games through out the day. These points can then be redeemed to win some fantastic prizes, from Premier League match tickets to match worn memorabilia!

·Exclusive Football Figure Release

These fantastic 9cm tall resin football figures of David Beckham, Wayne Rooney, David James and Steven Gerrard will only be available on the 27th March from the Football Fanatics. Limited to 750 pieces only and branded with Barnardo’s coloured base and special edition official England merchandise blister packaging, they are a must have item for any 3 Lions fan! PRODUCED UNDER LICENSE FROM ALEXANDER GLOBAL PROMOTIONS.

·Charity Auction

Amongst the signed items and tickets donated to us by football clubs and players are a pair of David Batty’s football boots, one of Lawrence Dallaglio's official signed England World Cup jerseys and a fully signed England shirt. Other interesting lots for football collector’s will be a Corinthian Stein Chelsea Master Model and 2 unreleased MicroStars of Thuram (Parma) and Van Der Sar (Juventus), kindly given to us by Nik Yeomans for auction along with a Jimmy Floyd Hasselbaink Chelsea match worn jersey.
