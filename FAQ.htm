Goalscorers - How do I enter them to get a Leading Goalscorers Table?

You need only add the names of players who score goals.
You only ever need to add a player's name once.
  1. Log In
  2. Click on Player in the upper menu
  3. Click on Add
  4. Enter the name - Surname first then forenames - of the goalscorer
  5. Date of Birth is optional
  6. Enter an unique player number - the number must not belong to any other player. It can be any number (except zero which is for Own Goals).
  7. Click on the Add button

You now need to register this player

 

You now need to record the player's appearance and goal(s)

  1. Click on the date of the match
  2. Click on the home or away team's name - it doesn't matter which
  3. Click on either the home or the away Team Sheet next to the score boxes
  4. Tick the corresponding "Played" boxes and also enter the number of goals in each "Goals" box.
  5. Click on OK. You can amend as much as you like so long as you finish off with a click on the OK button.


Deducting Points - How do I deduct points from a team?

You can add points to, or deduct points from, any team but there has to be an associated fixture.
You should not use this feature if a match is awarded as a walkover. In this case use the awarded game with no score buttons.
Points are usually adjusted in relation to a game when the result stands (goals scored are kept) but the table needs adjusting.

Example 1:
The home team won 4-1 but they fielded an ineligible player.
You decide to keep the score as a home win of 4-1 but give the points to the away team.
  1. Log In and get to the Update or Delete Fixture screen for the game.
  2. Scroll down to the Points Adjustment:  section:
  3. In this example you would select -3 for the home team and +3 for the away team.
  4. click on the Update button ( check the league table to see if you are satisfied with the outcome).
Example 2:
The home team failed to turn up on October 4th and you want to deduct 3 points from them.
The game will be played later on in the season but you don't have the rearranged date yet.
  1. Log In and get to the Update or Delete Fixture screen for the game.
  2. Scroll down to the Points Adjustment:  section:
  3. In this example you would select -3 for the home team.
  4. In the Notes section explain the situation fully and say there will be a date for the rearranged game announced soon.
  5. The score boxes should be empty
  6. click on the Update button ( check the league table to see if you are satisfied with the outcome).
  7. Later on you arrange to play the game on November 22nd.
  8. Change the fixture date from October 4th to November 22nd - do not delete the fixture and add a new fixture.
  9. Enter the original date of the game to the notes so everyone knows when the original deduction took place.

Removing a Team - How do I expunge their record?

If  a team has not played any games:
  1. Log In
  2. Delete all their fixtures
  3. Click on Constitution and select their Division
  4. Click on the team name and then click on the Delete button
  5. Leave them in any cup competitions they're entered in. Just award the games to their opponents.
If  a team has played any games it is important to keep the records to maintain a complete picture of the season for referees, goalscorers etc
  1. Email us with the details and he will run a program which will expunge their record. Everything is transferred to a Miscellaneous division.


Renaming a Team - one of my teams has changed its name, what do I do?

You can change the name of  a team at any time. This might be to correct the spelling or because of a basic name change.

At the beginning of a season a team might drop out and its place taken by another team. In this case you can just change the name to the new team so they inherit the old team's fixtures.

  1. Log In
  2. Click on Team
  3. Click on the team name
  4. Change the name and then click on the Update button

Cup Competition - I entered the results but the winning teams don't show in the next round. Why not?

You have a knockout competition with Match Numbers. You entered all the teams in the Constitution. You specified the home teams and the away teams and managed to schedule the games correctly.
You even put in the results but, the Winners of .... weren't replaced by the names of the winning teams.

Checklist
  1. Did you enter Winners of..... all the way to the semi finals?
  2. Did you fill in both columns? You need to enter both they have been drawn to play in match number ....and 
    if they win they will progress to match number ...     for every team all the way to the semi finals, if known.
Once you have remedied steps 1 and 2 go to the results you have entered and pretend to enter them again.
You only need to click on the Update button. The Winners of.... will be correctly replaced with the winning team's name.


I need to create two fixtures for the same team on the same day. How can I do this?

Double Headers - you are allowed  TeamA v TeamB  and  TeamB v TeamA  on the same day.

Otherwise, the system prevents you from double booking a team on the same day.

However, there are times when you'll want to override this restriction.
For example, TeamA might be involved in a County Cup, if really successful, on the same day as the more likely, ordinary league fixture.
You want to show both possibilities. You might want to show, for example, TeamA v TeamB on the same day as TeamA v TeamC
To do this...
  1. Schedule TeamA v TeamB in the normal way for the required date
  2. Schedule TeamA v TeamC on any other, free date
  3. Reschedule the free date to the required date and get a red warning message
  4. Go back and click on the Update button anyway - this forces it through.

Cup Competition - I can't get the Unscheduled Match for a pair of teams. Why not?

You have a knockout competition with Match Numbers. You entered a pair of teams in the Constitution by specifying the home team and the away team.
However, this pair of teams doesn't appear in Unscheduled Matches.

Checklist

  1. Has either team already been beaten in the competition? Only winning teams are considered for Unscheduled Matches.
  2. Has a team has been beaten but then been reinstated on a technicality?
  3. Does your cup competition have 1st and 2nd Leg matches in each round?

For 1. above, correct the earlier result then try again
For 2. above, add "IgnoreLosers" to the Division notes, create the fixture, then remove "IgnoreLosers".
For 3. above, add "IgnoreLosers" to the Division notes.

OR - You have a knockout competition without Match Numbers. You entered all participating teams in the Constitution.
However, one of the teams doesn't appear in Unscheduled Matches.

Checklist

  1. Has this team already been beaten in the competition? Only winning teams are considered for Unscheduled Matches.
